CEO Card Update Required — The Geologist

Current text (passive ability): "Tier 1 and Tier 2 ENERGY assets you own apply the GMI delta ×3 instead of ×2. Tier 3 ENERGY assets apply ×4 instead of ×3."

Problem: T3 Energy cards now have GMI ×3 as their printed baseline. The Geologist's hard-coded absolute values conflict with the card's own stated multiplier.

Proposed replacement text: "All ENERGY assets you own apply their GMI delta one additional time beyond their printed multiplier. A standard T1/T2 asset (×2) becomes ×3 under your ownership. A T3 asset (×3) becomes ×4. This applies to both positive and negative GMI deltas. Any other player holding an ENERGY asset you don't own uses the asset's printed multiplier as normal." This framing is relative to the card, not absolute — future T4 cards or variant rules won't break it.

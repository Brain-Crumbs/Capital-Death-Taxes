3 — Global Event Card · The Great Depression (Persistent)
PERSISTENT — stays face-up each year until escape roll
Global Event · Depression
The Great Depression
⟳ Depression
−3
GMI Delta
GMI −3 each year this card persists. All industries take the full delta per their own GMI mechanic.
Effects
STRESS · ALL PLAYERS
Each year this card persists: all players gain +1 stress at year end (before death rolls).
LOAN PRESSURE
Collateral violations this year cannot be resolved by selling to another player — forced buyback by the bank at current value only.
ESCAPE ROLL · EACH YEAR
Roll d6 at start of each year this card is active. On 4–6: depression ends, remove card. On 1–3: persists, all players +1 stress.
"Nobody saw it coming. Everybody said they did."
⑧ The persistent strip at the top is the first thing visible when the card is placed face-up on the table — unmissable reminder that this card doesn't go away.
⑨ The large GMI number is the primary mechanical fact. Players need it immediately when the card is flipped. Everything else is secondary.
⑩ Escape roll is the last effect block — players read it in resolution order: stress hits, then loan pressure, then roll to escape.
